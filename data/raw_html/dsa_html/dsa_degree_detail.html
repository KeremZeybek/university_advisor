DATA SCIENCE AND ANALYTICS UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM (BSDSA)
  Diploma Area Advisor: Enes Eryarsoy

The Data Science and Analytics Program offers an undergraduate education focused on the fundamentals of data science and modern data analysis techniques. Our interdisciplinary program prepares students from various fields such as engineering, natural sciences, social sciences, and management sciences to succeed in a data-driven world. To this end, our curriculum, supported by the Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences, the Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences, and the Sabancı Business School, provides a solid foundation in statistical analysis, machine learning, large language models, and data management. Students benefit from the interdisciplinary curriculum, which enables the analysis of data from different fields using modern techniques and the extraction of valuable insights from data.


*** Language courses offered by SL (School of Languages) are not included in the group of free elective courses.

Core Electives

The students will take at least 27 SU credits from the CORE course pool. Students are required to take at least 3 courses from FENS, 3 courses from FASS and 3 courses from SBS in this CORE course pool. The extra courses taken from this pool are directly counted towards "Area Elective" course requirements.
